📱 What data does the app process?

Uplift Daily stores your habit data (names, streaks, reminders) locally on your device and optionally in your personal iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. This data is never accessible to us.

  • Habit data – stored locally and optionally synced via your own iCloud (Apple CloudKit)
  • Your name – stored locally and in iCloud Key-Value Store for your avatar; never transmitted to us
  • Reminders – local push notifications, processed entirely on your device
  • HealthKit data – see dedicated section below

❤️ Apple Health (HealthKit)

Uplift reads health data (steps, workouts, active minutes) from Apple Health to automatically check off compatible habits. This requires your explicit permission via the iOS system dialog.

Apple-required disclosure:
  • HealthKit data is used exclusively to check off habits within Uplift — never for advertising or data-mining
  • HealthKit data is never sold or shared with third parties
  • HealthKit data is never stored on our servers — it remains on your device and in your personal iCloud
  • You can revoke Health access at any time: Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Uplift

🔥 Firebase Analytics

To understand whether the app is being used at all, we use Firebase Analytics by Google. We log a single anonymous event: app_started — triggered when the app launches. No personal identifiers, no habit content, no health data is included.

📊

Firebase Analytics

One anonymous event (app_started) is sent on launch. No personal data, no habits, no health information is transmitted. Data is processed by Google LLC in the USA.

Firebase Privacy Policy →

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – our legitimate interest in understanding basic app usage. Data is processed under Google's Data Processing Agreement with Standard Contractual Clauses for transfers to the USA.

🚫 What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect names, email addresses or location data
  • We do not create user profiles
  • We do not sell data to third parties
  • We do not use HealthKit data for advertising or data-mining
  • We do not use advertising networks or tracking pixels
  • We do not require account registration

☁️ iCloud Sync (CloudKit)

If iCloud is enabled on your device, your habit data is synced across your Apple devices via Apple CloudKit. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no access to your iCloud data.

🎤 Siri & Shortcuts

Uplift supports Siri and Apple Shortcuts so you can check off habits with your voice. All Siri processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store any voice data or Siri queries.
