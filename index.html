📱 What data does the app process?

Uplift Daily stores your habit data (names, streaks, reminders) locally on your device and optionally in your personal iCloud account. This data is never accessible to us.

  • Habit data – stored locally and optionally synced via your own iCloud (Apple CloudKit)
  • Reminders – local push notifications, processed only on your device
  • HealthKit data – read-only access to steps, workouts and activity (only with your explicit permission, never stored on our servers)

🔥 Firebase – Analytics & Crash Reports

To improve app stability and understand general usage patterns, we use two Firebase services by Google:

📊

Firebase Analytics

Collects anonymized, aggregated usage events (e.g. which screens are visited). No personal identifiers are transmitted. Data is processed by Google LLC in the USA.

Firebase Privacy Policy →
🛠️

Firebase Crashlytics

Records crash reports when the app crashes – including iOS version, device model and app version. No personal data (name, email, location) is included.

Firebase Privacy Policy →

Legal basis for Firebase processing: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – our legitimate interest in app stability and quality improvement. Data is processed under Google's Data Processing Agreement and subject to Standard Contractual Clauses for transfers to the USA.

🚫 What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect names, email addresses or location data
  • We do not create user profiles
  • We do not sell data to third parties
  • We do not use advertising networks or tracking pixels
  • We do not require account registration

☁️ iCloud Sync (CloudKit)

If iCloud is enabled on your device, your habit data is synced across your Apple devices via Apple CloudKit. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no access to your iCloud data.
